Questions

How does the free trial work?

Download PhoneStage and get 7 days of full access to every feature. No account or payment required. After the trial, subscribe to keep using Pro features.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel through your LemonSqueezy account and you keep access until the end of your billing period.

How is the license activated?

After subscribing, you receive a license key via email. Enter it in PhoneStage to unlock Pro features.

Do I need an internet connection?

Only for initial license activation and periodic validation. Recording, editing, and exporting all work offline.
