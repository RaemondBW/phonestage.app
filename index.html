How it works

Connect

Plug your iPhone into your Mac with a USB cable. PhoneStage detects it instantly.

2

Record

Hit record and use your phone normally. Touch events stream to the Mac via the companion app.

3

Edit

Use the timeline to trim, cut, and adjust speed. Fine-tune zoom, framing, and overlays.

4

Export

Render a polished video ready for social media, app store previews, or presentations.
