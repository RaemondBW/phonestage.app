Everything you need for a perfect demo

USB Screen Capture

Plug in your iPhone and record directly over USB. No AirPlay lag, no network drops, no iPhone Mirroring required.

Touch Visualization

Every tap, drag, and pinch is shown as a smooth halo overlay. Your audience sees exactly what you're doing.

Auto-Zoom

Automatically generates zoom plans from your gestures, focusing the camera where the action happens.

Timeline Editor

Trim, cut, and arrange your clips. Speed segments let you fast-forward through the boring parts.

Device Frames

Wrap your video in a realistic iPhone bezel with rounded corners and shadows for a professional look.

Custom Backgrounds

Choose solid colors, gradients, or blur effects. Set the aspect ratio and padding to fit any platform.

Camera Bubble

Add a webcam overlay to put a face to your demo. Drag it anywhere on the canvas.

Metal Export

GPU-accelerated rendering composites all overlays into a crisp final video at full resolution.

How it works

1

Connect

Plug your iPhone into your Mac with a USB cable. PhoneStage detects it instantly.

2

Record

Hit record and use your phone normally. Touch events stream to the Mac via the companion app.

3

Edit

Use the timeline to trim, cut, and adjust speed. Fine-tune zoom, framing, and overlays.

4

Export

Render a polished video ready for social media, app store previews, or presentations.
